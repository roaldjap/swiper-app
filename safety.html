Your Safety / Community Standards

We are a growing global portal where we encourage creative stories to be shared that is free Sent from my iPhone

We strive to provide a community that encourages stories to be shared amongst users to build diversity, awareness and inclusion and that users feel safe and non threatened.

Safety

We will remove content, disable accounts and work with local and international law enforcement when we feel there is a genuine risk to public safety.

Users can help by flagging any inappropriate content direct from the application.

Diversity in Topics and Opinion

Swiper encourages users to share their stories and thoughts as we promote and support global awareness. At times these topics, stories, and opinions are different from yours and may be uncomfortable. We try to strike a balance in the needs of our community and protect it by removing content that is graphic or insensitive and will disable accounts as needed.

Intellectual Property

You own all the content you post on Swiper and its visibility can be controlled by how you associate it to categories and tags. However when you post content you must be sure you have permission. You are responsible for respecting copyrights, trademarks and other legal matters / rights.
